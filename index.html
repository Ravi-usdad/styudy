About Study

At Study, our mission is clear: to provide a more sustainable alternative for moving around in Ibiza. By coming together and sharing our trips, we not only reduce the number of cars on the roads but also create a more engaged community with the island.

We promote collaborative mobility and provide you with the opportunity to meet and connect with other travelers or residents.

Why Study

Save time and avoid long waits

With Study, forget about endless queues to get a taxi or public transportation. Quickly find a driver willing to share their trip with you and reach your destination without delays.

Meet fellow travelers along the way

Study not only gets you to your destination but also provides an opportunity to meet new people and expand your network. Share stories, travel tips, and unforgettable experiences while exploring the beautiful island of Ibiza.

Travel more sustainably

With the increasing tourism in Ibiza, the island is affected by the excess of vehicles on the roads. By opting for shared trips through Study, you contribute to reducing the number of cars on the road, thus decreasing the environmental impact and promoting more sustainable mobility.

Avoid unnecessary expenses

Sharing the trip with other travelers gives you the opportunity to split the costs of fuel and tolls, resulting in significant savings on your transportation expenses. Travel economically and make the most of your budget on your island adventures
